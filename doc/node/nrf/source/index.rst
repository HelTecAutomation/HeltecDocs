Mesh Node series
================
:ht_translation:`[简体中文]:[English]`

Mesh Node series is a new series of development boards launched by Heltec based on the nRF52xxx MCU and SX-1262 LoRa chip. Compared to previous development boards, this series maintains strong long-distance communication capabilities while emphasizing low power consumption.
